Instituto para Desarrollo del Emprendimiento y Administración

About Us

The Institute for the Development of Entrepreneurship and Administration (IDEA) is a management school oriented to training leaders, professionals and entrepreneurs in order to develop skills and abilities that enable them to contribute with the success of their enterprise or business regardless of the economic sector where it is developed, either public or private. The institute will be guided by an academic offer of courses, workshops, seminars, diploma courses, postgraduate courses and executive training of international quality, under a teaching philosophy where the student is the center of the learning process.

The IDEA will start activities under the online modality aimed to entrepreneurs who want to start or promote their startup. For this, it will have fourth and fifth academic level professors from the most important universities in the world; adjunct professors (practitioners) and visiting professors. It is expected in the future to work with an hybrid format that allows the coexistence of distance and face-to-face education.

Nuestra Empresa

El Instituto para Desarrollo del Emprendimiento y Administración (IDEA), es una escuela de gerencia orientada a la formación de lideres, profesionales y empresarios con la finalidad de desarrollar competencias y habilidades que les permitan contribuir con el éxito de su emprendimiento o negocios sin importar el sector económico donde se desarrolla, tanto público como privado. EL instituto se orientará con una oferta académica de cursos, talleres, seminarios, diplomados, postgrados y formación ejecutiva de calidad internacional, bajo una filosofía de enseñanza donde el estudiante es el centro del proceso de aprendizaje.

El IDEA iniciará actividades bajo la modalidad online orientada a emprendedores que desean iniciar o impulsar su startup. Para ello, contará con profesores de cuarto y quinto nivel académico de las más importantes universidades del mundo; profesores adjuntos (practitioners) y profesores invitados. Se espera en el futuro migrar a un formato híbrido que permita la convivencia de las modalidades de educación a distancia y presencial.

Justification

Justificación

The Institute for the Development of Entrepreneurship and Administration (IDEA), was born in response to the absence of institutions dedicated to the transfer of knowledge of the entrepreneurial culture and the activities necessary to develop entrepreneurship. It is true that there are government and private institutions that seek to strengthen and support entrepreneurship. However, there is no institution fully dedicated to promoting the entrepreneurial culture from its foundations. Therefore, this initiative represents a new opportunity that will strengthen the entrepreneurial culture and provide knowledge to anyone interested in becoming and developing a business through easily accessible information that allows them to know the step-by-step process of conceiving an idea and materializing it. in a profitable and sustainable undertaking over time.

This institute hopes to be a reference in the promotion of entrepreneurial culture and support for entrepreneurship through the transfer of knowledge and the continuous support of viable business ideas that respond to the needs of society.

In this sense, the intention of the institution is to create strategic alliances with the productive and business sectors, non-profit organizations, school districts, institutes and universities, with the firm purpose of being an entity of change, introducing these concepts from an early age in education, to promote the importance of creative awakening, allowing in the near future the conception of new products, services and companies that promote self-employment, employment and the contribution to the state with new products, technology, taxes and many additional benefits that underlie the birth of a new company

El Instituto para desarrollo del Emprendimiento y Administración (IDEA), nace como respuesta a la ausencia de instituciones dedicadas a la transferencia de conocimientos de la cultura emprendedora y de las actividades necesarias para desarrollar emprendimientos. Si bien es cierto que existen instituciones gubernamentales y privadas que buscan fortalecer y apoyar el emprendimiento, no existe una institución volcada en su totalidad a fomentar la cultura emprendedora desde sus cimientos. Por ello, esta iniciativa representa una novedosa oportunidad que permitirá fortalecer la cultura emprendedora y brindar el conocimiento a toda persona interesada en convertirse y desarrollar un emprendimiento mediante información de fácil acceso que les permita conocer el paso a paso del proceso de concebir una idea y materializarla en un emprendimiento redituable y sustentable en el tiempo.

Este instituto espera ser referencia en la promoción de la cultura emprendedora y el apoyo a los emprendimientos mediante la transferencia de conocimiento y al apoyo continuo de ideas de negocio viables que respondan a necesidades de la sociedad.

En este sentido, la intención de la institución es crear alianzas estratégicas con los sectores productivos, empresariales, organizaciones sin fines de lucro, distritos escolares, institutos y universidades, con el firme propósito ser un ente de cambio, introduciendo estos conceptos desde temprana edad en la educación, para promover la importancia del despertar creativo, permitiendo en el futuro cercano la concepción de nuevos productos, servicios y empresas que fomenten el autoempleo, el empleo, la satisfacciones de necesidades y la contribución al estado en materia de nuevos productos, tecnología, impuestos y muchos beneficios adicionales que subyacen en el nacimiento de una nueva empresa.

Academic Offer

Oferta Académica

At IDEA we believe that anyone with the right knowledge is capable of building a great project that is profitable and has an economic and social impact. The programs, diplomas and courses to be offered can generate the fundations to promote an idea or a project in a short time.

  • Learn to undertake. Aimed at entrepreneurs who want to start their own business, but who still do not have a defined idea and need to validate their concept quickly and effectively, with the advice of certified mentors intended to validating the business idea in a fast and practical way, identifying the key elements of the business mode with evidence obtained in the market, which allow moving forward in a correct and less uncertain direction.
    Within this program, the following diploma courses and certifications will be developed to facilitate the entrepreneur to take a tour through the stages of entrepreneurship:
    1. Awakening the entrepreneur that lives in you. Intended to people interested in knowing the entrepreneurial culture, entrepreneurs and startups. This motivational course will allow the participant the empowerment of being. The intention is to get people to know themselves; exploring personal and professional interests. For this, it begins with the approach of a seminar entitled Learning to undertake, from the discursive dialectic in order to introduce and intertwine aspects of motivation and entrepreneurship. Within this program there are three areas that will be addressed as follows, managing personal development and entrepreneurial praxis through the identification of opportunities and the creativity of the participant.
      • Manage personal development. The aim is for the participant to be able to inquire about their objectives and sense of self, to know their abilities, developing a positive assessment of the characteristics that define them as a worker and a person inserted in a social environment. This program is intended to the participant to ask questions that allow easy insertion into the entrepreneurial culture. For this, the use of questions is preponderant since they allow the person answers from their being; allowing them to guide their social actions in search of a dream, their dream. It is directed to set key aspects in the participant for development as an entrepreneur, such as: being successful does not make you happy, but being happy leads you directly to success, especially when the person decides to do what they are passionate about without excuses. It is necessary to promote answers to transcendental questions in the participant that can induce labor mobility, starting from the identification of their dream and going through the following: what are they passionate about and what gives them happiness, where they are at this moment. what motivation and happiness gives them the work he does, how much he gets paid for giving up his dream; You have a dream, what prevents you from doing it.
      • How to identify opportunities. The idea that guides this course is to facilitate the understanding that undertaking requires an observation process in the participant. The analysis of the context and the changes that occur is essential to identify needs, obstacles and opportunities. What is relevant is to evidence the theory in the praxis, allowing the participant to understand the importance of observation as a methodology that facilitates the identification of entrepreneurship opportunities.
      • How to be creative. Intended to promoting creativity from praxis, making use of exemplifications and questions that facilitate responses from the participants and for the participants, whether they are future entrepreneurs, entrepreneurs or startups. The interest of the course is to be a catalyst to promote the creative action of the participant through practical examples that provide new solutions and satisfy their own needs or those of the environment. In this stage, examples of local, national and international enterprises are used, people who left formal jobs to pursue a dream, undertake and become successful entrepreneurs, serving as a practical example and complement.
    2. From the idea to the business plan. This course is aimed at future entrepreneurs, entrepreneurs and startups with the purpose of providing a vision that allows them to know the process of formalizing the business idea through theoretical - practical aspects so the participant can learn the process of formalizing the business idea and lead the enterprise to success. The intention is to facilitate the step by step; from the conception of the idea, through the structuring of the business plan to its implementation and start-up. It is expected that participants who are evaluating the possibility of a business venture and have different doubts about entrepreneurship such as: can it be a good business? Who and how should I orient myself commercially? What legal form is the most appropriate? for my business project? How should I organize myself? What financial aspects should I know? What is my break-even point to be profitable? Among other aspects of interest. This course includes different topics: from ideas to an action plan, what business to set up and promoting your own business. It is expected for the development of the activities of this course to complement with courses and seminars in the respective areas that contemplate the business plan such as: organization and systems, marketing, legal and financial advice of interest in organizational management and entrepreneurship.
    3. From ideas to an action plan. Is geired towards future entrepreneurs, entrepreneurs and startups to provide step by step how to translate the idea into a plan, evaluating its feasibility in different scenarios and the risks associated with its execution. The participants will understand that entrepreneurship is a process that requires the search and allocation of resources which make the objectives viable. The intention is to make the participant conceptually familiar with the business plan and its structure, as well as the recognition of the importance of the plan for entrepreneurship and its transition to success. The approach includes theoretical and practical aspects related to: what the business plan is and what it is for, description of the business, the general environment and the sector, among others, such as:
      • Validation route of a business idea: Aimed at knowing the importance of creating a business plan for the development of the business idea and entrepreneurship.
        • The business plan: the new vision of this essential tool for any entrepreneurial project.
        • Business Model: Formulation of a business model for the new venture or startup.
        • Generating value in your business.
      • Marketing at the service of the entrepreneur. Directed to knowing the importance of marketing management and its strategies for the development and consolidation of the business idea.
        • Market Segment, Target Market and how to identify it.
        • From the idea to the value proposition; The product and its versions.
        • How to determine the price of the product.
        • Distribution channels; Interfaces between the product and the clients;
        • Relationships: types and their effectiveness, depending on the phase of the idea.
        • Revenue streams: how do customers prefer to pay? Criteria for selection
      • Business efficiency: Aimed at knowing the key resources: what to have and when to have it.
      • Key activities: what must be done and constantly measured.
      • Key allies: types of alliances and their objectives
      • Cost structures: what remains at the time of execution the idea of a business
      • The opportunity in numbers. Intended to gain the knowledge of the technical-financial feasibility analysis.
      • Minimum conditions of economic viability: investment, income, costs and liquidity.
      • The initial investment: how to estimate it.
      • Financing: where and how to obtain it, Forecast cash flows.
      • The environment and its risks: Directed to knowing the strategy applied to the business model; Pricing and stable and variable scenarios.
      • The Minimum Viable Team: Aimed at knowing the importance of building your work team. Innovation in business models, Entrepreneurship alone or with partners, Required team, How to divide equity.
  • HomeSchools Entrepreneur. Directed to entrepreneurs to train in digital transformation and business growth in online sessions via streaming. This program seeks to become the specialized space for online training through webinars, programs, diplomas and courses taught by leaders of the entrepreneurial ecosystem and business experts from all sectors, where the participant can receive mentoring and key teachings to promote an idea of business or project. The topics will be technology, logistics, human capital, among others.
  • Digital strategies to boost your business. Aimed at entrepreneurs and personal brands who want to leverage digital media and understand digital media and the possibilities they offer to boost business. It seeks to publicize the main digital platforms and applications that will support you to promote your project, company or personal brand.
  • The new commercial management. Strategies and digital resources for advertising and promoting entrepreneurship. Social Networks as an entrepreneurial leverage strategy.
  • Magnament program. Aimed at entrepreneurs involved in making strategic decisions who hold positions of responsibility, and whose careers are directed towards the highest positions in their startup, interested in obtaining a systemic vision of the organization, knowing the latest trends in global management, and developing skills to grow professionally and grow your business or startup.
  • Management and Negotiation. Aimed at entrepreneurs and startups in order to deepen the practice of negotiation, building strategic alliances, achieving high-value internal and external agreements to strengthen the business idea, entrepreneurship or startup with the purpose of developing skills and abilities for managerial decision making, respond quickly to challenges, opportunities and threats, you will make effective connections, close deals successfully, understand the underlying interests of the parties and strategically make trade-offs.
  • Supply Chain and Logistics. Aimed at entrepreneurs so that they relate, reflect, distinguish, formulate and evaluate how their entrepreneurial project or startup works, in order to maximize their performance and results. Oriented to the search to optimize and improve business management and understand how the new digital economy impacts logistics processes.

Program designed in certifiable modules until becoming a diploma, aimed at entrepreneurs who seek to learn first-hand the key aspects in the creation and development of a company, including: Innovation, Attracting key human talent, Brand Positioning and Healthy Finances.
  • Customer Experience: Beyond Service. Aimed at entrepreneurs to provide an overview of Customer Experience Management (CXM), allowing them to understand the best practices for the customer, aimed at developing a customer-centric culture in order to provide value and further promote the sustainability of the business or startup. This course provides principles and tools of the Customer Experience, achieving an understanding how to satisfy the needs, exceed their expectations, captivate, surprise and be able to continue generating emotional connection with customers.
  • Healthy financial education online. Aimed at entrepreneurs to develop skills and knowledge that will allow them to know how to generate a budget for the short and long term, create strategies to respond to liabilities, learn how to obtain insurance, plan how to finance purchases, develop an investment plan, design a plan finances and prepare a savings plan.
  • Quality, Productivity and Competitiveness. Aimed at entrepreneurs to develop skills and knowledge that will allow them to be able to identify the factors to maintain and improve Quality, Productivity and achieve Excellence in products and services, to put them into practice in their respective ventures.
  • Quality of service and customer service. Aimed at providing the participant with improvement tools that allow them to manage complaints and claims and build customer loyalty, considering that all business initiatives must be attentive to customer needs and provide a quality service that exceeds their expectations, incorporating tools in order to help them to enhance their capabilities to provide attentive, fast and quality customer service. Within the topic to be addressed and that is relevant for every entrepreneur is: how to address claims, anticipate and handle complaints, develop attitudes that contribute to greater customer satisfaction, manage conflictive situations and mediate to offer solutions, identify indicators of the claims, their management and continuous control in order to learn from the situation and then leverage it as an opportunity. Know concepts, experiences and practices linked to how to manage customer perceptions with the intention of satisfying them and building sustainable relationships that are sustainable over time.

Este curso esta dirigido a futuros emprendedores, emprendedores y startup con la finalidad de proporcionar una visión que les permita conocer el proceso de formalizar la idea de negocio a través de aspectos teórico - prácticos que permiten al participante conocer el proceso de formalizar la idea de negocio y conducir el emprendimiento al éxito. La intención es facilitar el paso a paso; desde la concepción de la idea, pasando por la estructuración del plan de negocio hasta su implantación y puesta en marcha. Se espera que los participantes que están valorando la posibilidad de una aventura empresarial y tienen diferentes dudas sobre el emprendimiento tales como: ¿puede ser un buen negocio?, ¿a quién y cómo debo orientarme comercialmente?, ¿qué forma jurídica es la más adecuada para mi proyecto de negocio?, ¿cómo debo organizarme?, ¿qué aspectos financieros debo conocer?, ¿cuál es mi punto de equilibrio para ser rentable?, entre otros aspectos de interés. Este curso comprende diferentes momentos a saber: de las ideas a un plan de acción, qué negocio montar e impulsando tu propio negocio. Se espera para el desarrollo de las actividades de esta curso complementar con cursos y seminarios en las respectivas áreas que contemplan el plan de negocio como: organización y sistemas, mercadotecnia, asesoría legal, financiera, entre otras, de interés en la gestión organizacional y emprendimiento.
    3. De las ideas a un plan de acción. Dirigido a futuros emprendedores, emprendedores y startup para brindar el paso a paso de como traducir la idea en un plan, evaluando su factibilidad en distintos escenarios y los riesgos asociados a su ejecución. Los participantes comprenderán que emprender es un proceso que requiere la búsqueda y asignación de recursos que viabilicen los objetivos. La intencionalidad es lograr que el participante se familiarice conceptualmente con el plan de negocio y su estructura, así como el reconocimiento de la importancia del plan para el emprendimiento y su tránsito al éxito. El abordaje contempla aspectos teórico prácticos relacionados con: qué es y para qué sirve el plan de negocio, descripción del negocio, el entorno general y el sector, entre otros, como:
      • Ruta de validación de una idea de negocio: Dirigido a conocer la importancia de crear un plan de negocios para el desarrolla de la idea de negocios y el emprendimiento.
        • El plan de negocio: la nueva visión de esta fundamental herramienta para todo proyecto de emprendimiento.
        • Modelo de Negocio: Formulación de un modelo de negocio para el nuevo emprendimiento o startup.
        • Generando valor en tu emprendimiento.
      • Mercadotecnia al servicio del emprendedor. Dirigido a conocer la importancia de la gestión de la mercadotecnia y sus estrategias para el desarrollo y consolidación de la idea de negocio.
        • Segmento de Mercado, Mercado Meta y cómo identificarlo.
        • De la idea a la propuesta de valor; El producto y sus versiones.
        • Cómo determinar el precio del producto.
        • Los canales de distribución; Interfaces entre el producto y los clientes;
        • Las relaciones: tipos y su efectividad dependiendo de la fase de la idea.
        • Las vías de ingreso: ¿cómo prefieren pagar los clientes? Criterios para la selección.
      • La eficiencia del Negocio: Dirigido a conocer los recursos clave: qué tener y cuándo tenerlo, Las actividades clave: qué se debe hacer y medir constantemente, Aliados clave: tipos de alianzas y sus objetivos, Estructuras de costos: qué resta al momento de ejecutar la idea de un negocio.
      • La oportunidad en números.: Dirigido a conocer el análisis de viabilidad Técnico-financiera. Condiciones mínimas de viabilidad económica: inversión, ingresos, costos y liquidez, La inversión inicial: cómo estimarla, El financiamiento: dónde y cómo conseguirlo, Prever flujos de efectivo.
      • El entorno y sus riesgos: Dirigido a conocer la estrategia aplicada al modelo de negocio; Fijación de precios y, Escenarios estables y variables.
      • El Equipo mínimo viable: Dirigido a conocer la importancia de construir tu equipo de trabajo. La innovación en los modelos de negocio, Emprendimiento en solitario o con socios, Equipo requerido, Cómo dividir el patrimonio (equity).

Se busca dar a conocer las principales plataformas digitales y aplicaciones que te apoyarán para impulsar tu proyecto, empresa o marca personal.

Dirigido a emprendedores y startup con la finalidad de profundizar en la práctica de la negociación, construyendo alianzas estratégicas, logrando acuerdos internos y externos de alto valor para fortalecer la idea de negocio, el emprendimiento o su startup, con el propósito de desarrollar competencias y habilidades para la toma de decisiones gerenciales, responder con celeridad a retos, oportunidades y amenazas, lograrás conexiones efectivas, cerrar acuerdos de manera exitosa, comprenderás los intereses subyacentes de las partes y hacer concesiones de manera estratégica.

Dirigido a emprendedores para proporcionar una visión general sobre el Customer Experience Management (CXM), permitiendo entender las mejores prácticas para el cliente, orientadas al desarrollo de una cultura centrada en el cliente con el objeto de brindar valor y promover aún más la sostenibilidad del emprendimiento o startup.   • Calidad de Servicio y Atención al Cliente. Dirigido a brindar al participante herramientas de mejora que permiten gestionar quejas y reclamos y fidelizar clientes, considerando que toda iniciativa de negocio debe estar atenta a las necesidades del cliente y brindar un servicio de calidad que supere sus expectativas, incorporando herramientas que les permitan potenciar sus capacidades para brindar un servicio atento, rápido y de calidad en la atención a los clientes. Dentro de la temática a ser abordada y que resulta de relevancia para todo emprendedor, se encuentra: cómo atender reclamos, anticipar y manejar quejas, desarrollar actitudes que contribuyan a una mayor satisfacción del cliente,gestionar situaciones conflictivas y mediar para ofrecer soluciones, identificar indicadores de los reclamos, su gestión y continuo control a efectos de lograr aprender de la situación y luego apalancarla como oportunidad. Conocer conceptos, experiencias y prácticas vinculadas a la forma de gestionar las percepciones del cliente con la intención de satisfacerlo y construir relaciones sustentables y sostenibles en el tiempo.
